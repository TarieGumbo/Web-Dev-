What is Self Care anyway?

Self Care is essentially what it sounds like: taking care of yourself. Its any activity or practice that's embraced soly for the purpose of one's wellbeing.

Self care comes in all forms: making nourishing meals, doing exerciese, meitating, spending time in nature, oil pulling or treating oneself to a facial. Its not one specific practice but anything is done to nourish the body or mind.

Why do we need to take care of ourselves

It is very common phenomenon to get caught up in daily life - work, kids, relationships, errands and family commitments - that we leave little time for ourselves. The mornings are opposite of leisurely as we hop in and out of the shower, smudge makeup in the care and wolf down breakfast at our desk.

If we are a parent, we so lovingly devote all of our attention to our children that we barely have time to wash our hair. Whether we spend the day at work or with our kids, it can easily fly by in a flurry of meeting deadlines

Evenings are equally as rushed with just enough time to slap dinner together or grab something to go on the way home. There's no real transition between the evening's activities and the day's work: brush teeth and hit the pillow. Wash, rinse, repeat.

They feel as if there is no time for self-care. But without making an effort to care for their wellbeing, they end up feeling worn out, lacking energy, and plagued by pesky physical ailments like gas and bloating, which are not only uncomfortable but can lead to bigger problems. Life feels like a whirlwind. It's easy to lose sight of the bigger picture when life is lived without pause.
